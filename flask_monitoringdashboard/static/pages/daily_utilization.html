Information
Graph

You can hover the graph with your mouse to see the actual values. You can also use the buttons at the top of the graph to select a subset of graph, scale it accordingly or save the graph as a PNG image.

Axes

The X-axis presents the amount of requests. The Y-axis presents a number of days.

Content

This graph presents a horizontal stacked barplot. Each endpoint is represented by a color. In the legend on the right, you can disable a certain endpoint by clicking on it. You can also show in the information of a single endpoint by double clicking that endpoint in the legend. The information from this graph can be used to see on which days (a subset of) the endpoints are used to most.
